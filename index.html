What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money, or interrupting business processes.

To learn cybersecurity, one should focus on networking, programming (Python is highly recommended), ethical hacking, and understanding security tools & frameworks.
